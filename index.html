About VoxCare AI

VoxCare AI is a healthcare technology platform that delivers voice-first automation to medical practices across the United States. Our platform handles patient communications, appointment scheduling, and clinical intake through advanced voice AI, enabling healthcare providers to focus on patient care rather than administrative overhead.

Built with production-grade infrastructure including Twilio voice integration, Whisper speech recognition via Groq, and ElevenLabs text-to-speech, VoxCare AI processes thousands of patient interactions with HIPAA-aware security controls. The platform integrates seamlessly with existing EHR systems and scheduling workflows, requiring minimal setup while delivering immediate operational impact.

Founded and engineered by Janagam Bharath, VoxCare AI serves small to mid-sized clinics, family practices, and telehealth providers who require reliable, compliant automation without enterprise complexity. Our system is designed for the realities of modern healthcare delivery‚Äîwhere every patient call matters and operational efficiency directly impacts care quality.

VoxCare AI operates at the intersection of conversational AI and healthcare operations, providing clinics with the automation capabilities previously available only to large hospital systems. We believe that advanced technology should be accessible to practices of all sizes, which is why we've built a platform that combines enterprise-grade reliability with straightforward implementation.
