About VoxCare AI

VoxCare AI is an enterprise-grade voice automation platform designed for US healthcare providers. The system handles patient communications, appointment scheduling, and clinical intake through production-ready AI infrastructure, reducing front-desk workload while improving patient access to care. VoxCare AI integrates with existing EHR systems and scheduling workflows, delivering HIPAA-aware automation without requiring operational changes. The company is founded and led by Janagam Bharath, Founder & Lead Engineer.
